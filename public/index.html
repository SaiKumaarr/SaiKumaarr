Custom resume format

The converter expects plain text files that follow the structure below. Lines beginning with - or * are treated as list items. Empty lines are ignored. 

Name: Ada Lovelace
Email: ada@example.com
Phone: +44 0000 0000
Summary:
- Mathematician and writer with a passion for computation.
Experience:
* Analytical Engines Ltd | Consultant | 1843-1845
Education:
* University of London | Mathematics | 1835
Skills:
- Analytical Thinking
- Programming Languages: Sketching Engines
