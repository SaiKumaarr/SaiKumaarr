AI Product Manager Portfolio Mockups

This standalone HTML preview mirrors the interactive wireframes built in the Next.js application. Use it to quickly share or review the visual direction for the hero, skills, professional journey, AI experiments, tools, and contact flows without running the development server.

Hero · Who I Am

Immersive identity moment

Split layout hero pairing a personal brand statement with animated role ticker and an orbiting portrait treatment that conveys motion and focus.

Brand Narrative

“AI Product Manager with 7+ years orchestrating data-driven products from concept to scalable launch. Mission: design AI that amplifies human intelligence responsibly.”

Primary CTAs: View My Work · Book a Strategy Session

Motion Treatment

Circular portrait with gradient ring using the slow-spin accent, paired with hover overlay revealing quick facts such as responsible AI focus and experiment volume.

Animated ticker cycles titles: Product Strategist → AI Experimenter → Team Catalyst.

Skills & Expertise

Interactive competency map

Tabbed skill stacks visualise proficiency across technical, product management, AI/ML, and soft skills with radial meters and project filters.

Segmented navigation

  • Technical · APIs, SQL, Python, LLM orchestration (5+ years).
  • Product · Vision setting, roadmapping, stakeholder alignment.
  • AI/ML · Prompt engineering, model evaluation, responsible AI.
  • Soft Skills · Cross-functional leadership, storytelling, experimentation culture.

Micro interactions

Cards flip on hover/tap to reveal use cases and link to relevant experiments. Progress arcs animate when scrolled into view.

Skill tags filter connected experiments surfaced beneath the grid.

Professional Journey

Seven-year growth timeline

Horizontal timeline (desktop) and stacked milestones (mobile) highlight logos, durations, quantified impact, and testimonials.

Milestone breakdown

  • Company nodes expand to reveal KPIs, team size, and strategic wins.
  • Transitions callouts explain role changes and promotion context.
  • Logos start monochrome and glow with brand color on interaction.

Measurement-first storytelling

Achievements spotlight metrics such as +35% model adoption, 3 AI features launched, 0→1 experimentation programs, and user impact counts.

Expandable drawers use focus traps for keyboard accessibility.

AI Experiments Showcase

Filterable gallery of prototypes

Masonry grid features image generation, advanced prompting, and supporting tools. Cards open to method, datasets, and outcomes with optional demos.

Gallery highlights

  • Before/after sliders for Midjourney and Stable Diffusion image explorations.
  • Prompt playground replicates LLM chains with guardrail notes.
  • Tags show models, latency, and business impact metrics.

Filtering & context

Filters by category and tooling instantly update the layout. Each experiment surfaces methodology, learnings, and next steps for recruiters to assess depth.

Modal layout supports light/dark mode with accessible contrast ratios.

AI Tools & Technologies

Capability-aligned tool matrix

Responsive grid or carousel grouping tools by function: Image Generation, NLP, Data Analysis, and Product Operations.

Tool highlights

  • OpenAI APIs, LangChain, Pinecone, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion.
  • Analytics and product ops with Mixpanel, Amplitude, Notion, Jira.
  • Design stack via Figma and FigJam with AI-assisted ideation.

Interaction details

Hover reveals proficiency level and signature use case snippet. Clicking filters relevant experiments or timeline moments that prove application depth.

Supports swipe gestures on mobile for carousel presentation.

Contact & Collaboration

High-conversion outreach hub

Two-column layout featuring narrative CTA, primary contact form, and instant links to email, LinkedIn, and Calendly.

Form structure

  • Name, email, project type dropdown, message field, optional budget slider.
  • Inline validation with accessible messaging and ARIA attributes.
  • Success confirmation with subtle confetti micro-interaction.

Supporting channels

Dedicated buttons to email, LinkedIn (saikumarbasole), and calendar booking. Mobile responsive with sticky bottom CTA bar.

Footer captures newsletter opt-in and privacy-friendly analytics notice.
